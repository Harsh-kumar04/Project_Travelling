Punducherry

The calm and colourful Union Territory of Puducherry lies on India's southern coast. A former French colony, it is known for age-old monuments, majestic churches, exotic beaches, and tree-lined boulevards.

How to reach?

Pondicherry is best accessible by road, either by bus or by taxi. Private tourist buses ply from Chennai, Madurai and Bengaluru to Pondicherry. Busses also connect Pondicherry to Thanjavur, Trichy, Chidamabaram and Coimbatore.
